SEVENTEEN


SEVENTEEN, also known as SVT, is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment. The group debuted on May 26, 2015, with 13 members who are divided into three sub-units: Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit, and Performance Unit. Known for their synchronized choreography and self-producing abilities, SEVENTEEN has gained a massive following worldwide.
